About Us


Like a kid in a candy store, that is exactly how all of our customers feel. Whether they are 2 or 92, we bring the kid out of everyone. With locations all over the nation, Candy World brings the little joys of childhood back into the homes of adults and creates lifelong memories for children. Integrating excitement with a wide offering of high quality items, Candy World brings its customers over 600 varieties of bulk candies, gift packages, children’s items, and other rare products (including Nostalgic items such as Teaberry Gum, Big Hunk, etc).

We bring the customer over 25+ years of experience combined with great customer service to provide the ultimate shopping experience. Each of our stores feature an extensive assortment of gummies, chocolates - including selections of gourmet and fine European chocolates, sugar free chocolates and candies, sour candies, and of course the widely popular Jelly Belly. The warm, brightly colored environment is a welcoming atmosphere for all to enjoy. We bring every imaginable kind of candy straight to our customers fingertips and introduce customers to an unimaginable world of candies. We focus on customer satisfaction, with a strong emphasis on the freshness and quality of our products. We believe that we can match every one of our customers desires, whether it is looking for a simple brand of candy or assembling an intricate gift for loved ones. We carry seasonal products as well as holiday specials. We plan, buy, and sell with only one thing in mind, the fulfillment of our customers’ desires!

Candy World carries a wide variety of corporate gift products, bulk candies, children novelties, imported and domestic gourmet chocolates, sugar-free chocolates and candies, fudge, truffles, chocolate gift boxes, and other confectionary related gift items. We also carry gift mugs, gift baskets, liquor chocolates, in-demand popular character plushes, beanie babies, popcorn, and much more. We will do all we can to cater to the needs of children, adults, senior citizens, and corporate clients.

The warm, brightly colored environment will be a welcoming atmosphere for all to enjoy. We believe that Candy World will be able to match every one of its requests, whether it’s looking for a small pack of gum or assembling an intricate gift for a loved one. We buy and sell with only one thing in mind, the satisfaction of our customers. Candy World attracts more customers by providing a vast selection of seasonal products as well as holiday specials and superior customer service. We focus on customer satisfaction, and our commitment to customer service will be one of the top priorities of Candy World. Our employees will be knowledgeable about all of our products to the extent that they will be able to explain the contents of the product and the process through which the product was made. Candy World will not only focus on in-store purchases but also on phone orders, and provide a shipping service to honor orders as requested by our customers.
